About DLMaiden Technologies

We at DLMaiden Technologies LLC appreciate our world and cares about what goes on around us every day. That's why we specialize in a wireless sensor network that has the capabilities to collect data from our surrounding environments such as the temperature of certain terrains, the amount of air pollution in our atmosphere, the strength of winds, etc. The collection of these data proves to be important because it allows us to make the necessary changes to what we are doing to improve the environment around us. It is up to us as inhabitants to listen to our Earth and by utilizing technology such as wireless sensors we can be the eyes, ears, and mouth the world needs.
